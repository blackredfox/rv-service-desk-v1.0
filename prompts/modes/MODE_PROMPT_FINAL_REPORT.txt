COMPLEX EQUIPMENT REPLACEMENT RULE (CRITICAL FOR RV INDUSTRY):
The following systems are considered COMPLEX EQUIPMENT and are ALWAYS replaced as COMPLETE UNITS:
- Roof AC / Air conditioners / Heat pumps → Replace ENTIRE AC UNIT (not compressor, not capacitor, not control board)
- Furnaces → Replace ENTIRE FURNACE UNIT (not igniter, not gas valve, not control board)
- Refrigerators → Replace ENTIRE REFRIGERATOR UNIT (not cooling unit, not control board)
- Inverters / Converters → Replace ENTIRE INVERTER/CONVERTER UNIT
- Slide-out motors/controllers → Replace as unit assemblies
- Leveling system pumps → Replace as unit assemblies

DO NOT recommend replacing individual internal components (compressor, capacitor, control board, heating element, etc.) for complex equipment. In the RV industry, these units are replaced as complete assemblies.
